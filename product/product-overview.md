# Wolf Goat Pig

## Description
A digital companion for the classic Wolf Goat Pig golf betting game from Wing Point Golf & Country Club. The app handles the complex betting rules, tracks scores and winnings in real-time, and helps players understand optimal strategy - so your group can focus on the golf and the fun.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Complex betting rules are hard to track manually
Automatically handles intricate calculations (doubles, wagering phases, team formations, special bets) so players can focus on the game.

### Problem 2: Paper scorecards are tedious and error-prone
Comprehensive digital tracking of scores, betting actions, and running totals for each player.

### Problem 3: Newcomers struggle to learn the game
Real-time betting odds, expected value calculations, and strategic reasoning help players understand and improve.

### Problem 4: Settling up at the end is confusing
Clear visualization of who owes what, with complete game history.

### Problem 5: Club betting traditions are hard to pass on
Preserves the authentic Wing Point rules and makes them accessible to new players.

## Key Features
- Real-time scorekeeper for live golf rounds
- Automatic betting calculations (The Float, The Option, doubles, special rules)
- Team formation with captain selection and partnership tracking
- Betting odds with probability analysis and strategic recommendations
- Google Sheets integration for live score sync
- Complete game history and leaderboards
